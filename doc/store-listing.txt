Full description:
Your memory extension. AIS is a fast, private index for the things you want to
find again: links, documents, notes, and passwords, filed under your own
keywords and recalled by any combination of them.

Everything stays on your device in plain text you can read and export. Secrets
are encrypted. No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no servers. Optional sync is
peer-to-peer between your own devices over your local network, end to end
encrypted.

Built on a small C engine for instant startup and search over very large
collections, the same engine behind the desktop, web, and command-line versions.

- File anything under your own tags, recall by any combination.
- Plain-text storage you own and can export; encrypted secrets.
- No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no cloud.
- Optional encrypted peer-to-peer sync across your devices.
- Fast even at a lifetime of entries.
